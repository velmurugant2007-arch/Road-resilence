# Judge Requirement Matrix

**Purpose**: To map every aspect of the project directly to the expectations of the ISRO evaluation committee.

| Evaluation Criterion | Judge Expectation | Evidence We Must Provide | Feature Satisfying It | Documentation Supporting It | Demonstration Strategy | Risks if Missing |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Problem Understanding** | Deep comprehension of "fragmentation" and "spectral blindness". | Mathematical proof that standard AI fails. | Topology-aware loss; Topological Cleaning module. | PS-4 Analysis Report; Research Notebook. | Side-by-side visual comparison (Standard AI vs. Our Pipeline). | Seen as just another generic semantic segmentation project. |
| **Scientific Correctness** | Robust application of Graph Theory. | Accurate calculation of BC, k-Core, etc. | Graph Intelligence Engine (NetworkX implementation). | Graph Theory Guide; Centrality comparison tables. | Interactive display of critical bottlenecks with clear legends. | Solution deemed unscientific or mathematically flawed. |
| **Engineering Excellence** | Scalable, reliable, decoupled architecture. | Clean code, modular pipeline, handled edge cases. | Pre-computed "Hero City" for zero-latency. | Master Design Document; Architecture Handbook. | A perfectly fluid, crash-free, zero-latency live demo. | Demo crashes or times out (Fatal error). |
| **Real-World Utility** | Applicable to disaster response and urban planning. | Realistic simulation of urban collapse. | Spatially-Correlated Disruption Tool (Flood/Earthquake). | Competitor Intelligence Report (justifying bounding box vs random). | Judge draws a disaster zone; network heals instantly. | Project feels like an academic toy, not an ISRO tool. |
| **Documentation & Traceability** | Professional, ISRO-grade engineering rigor. | ADRs, Decision Logs, Risk Registers. | The Project Memory System. | Developer Handbook; The entire `memory/` directory. | Walk the judges through the GitHub repo structure briefly. | Seen as a quick hack rather than a deployable system. |
| **Innovation** | Moving beyond the basic prompt requirements. | Something unexpected but highly relevant. | Confidence-Weighted Routing / Gamified Dashboard. | Innovation Matrix; Future Ideas log. | Showcasing the "Confidence" layer overlay on the map. | Average score; fails to stand out against 90% of teams. |
