# Presentation Strategy

## Project ATLAS — Route Resilience
### Knowledge Base Entry

> Presentation design, storytelling structure, and demo flow for the ISRO judging panel.

---

## Status

> ⚠️ Presentation preparation begins in **Phase 11**.

## Presentation Philosophy

1. **Tell a story** — Problem → Approach → Innovation → Results → Impact
2. **Show, don't tell** — Live demo or compelling visualizations over slides
3. **Scientific credibility** — Every claim backed by evidence
4. **Engineering depth** — Architecture decisions explained, not just shown
5. **Innovation highlight** — What makes this solution different from others

## Anticipated Structure

1. Problem Context (1-2 min) — The real-world significance
2. Technical Approach (3-4 min) — Architecture, algorithms, key decisions
3. Innovation (1-2 min) — What we do differently
4. Live Demo (3-4 min) — Interactive demonstration
5. Results & Metrics (1-2 min) — Quantitative evaluation
6. Future Scope (1 min) — Scalability and real-world deployment
