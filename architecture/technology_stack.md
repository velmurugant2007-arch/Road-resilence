# Technology Stack

## Project ATLAS — Route Resilience

> ⚠️ **Status**: Awaiting Phase 5. Technology selections will be documented here with full justification.

## Selection Criteria

Every technology choice must be justified against:

1. **Fitness for purpose** — Does it solve our specific problem well?
2. **Team familiarity** — Can we use it effectively within hackathon time constraints?
3. **Community support** — Is it well-documented with active community?
4. **Integration** — Does it work well with other selected technologies?
5. **Performance** — Does it meet our performance requirements?
6. **License** — Is it permissively licensed?
7. **Longevity** — Will it be maintained beyond the hackathon?

---

*Technology stack decisions will be made during Phase 5 after research is complete.*
